About Us

Detail about our bureau

Welcome to the Addis Ababa Bureau of Finance, your trusted partner in financial management and economic development in Ethiopia's vibrant capital city. Established with the mission to ensure fiscal responsibility, transparency, and sustainable growth, our bureau plays a pivotal role in managing the city's finances, fostering economic stability, and enhancing the overall quality of life for its residents.

With a dedicated team of professionals and a commitment to excellence, the Addis Ababa Bureau of Finance oversees a wide range of financial activities, including budget planning and implementation, revenue generation, expenditure management, financial reporting, and strategic financial analysis. We work tirelessly to ensure that public funds are utilized efficiently and effectively to support key priorities such as infrastructure development, social welfare programs, education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability.

Mission / Vision / Values

Mission

By carrying out fiscal policy and income research, by making participatory and fair budget allocation, by following up and evaluating the performance of government and civil society organizations projects

Vision

Addis Ababa City Administration Finance Bureau City Administration Council's vision is to see the city become one of the cities with a high middle income by 2022 by efficiently managing the city's resources and ensuring rapid, sustainable and comprehensive development

Values

  • Transparency, accountability and fairness
  • Providing superior service
  • Knowledge management and readiness for change
  • Participatory and teamwork
  • For resource efficiency and development activities
